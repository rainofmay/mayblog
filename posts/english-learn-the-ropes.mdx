✏️ **`Know/Learn the ropes. : ~ 하는 법, 요령을 배우다. `**
<br />
> A(a team manager) : How are you doing with your Blender project?
> B(a designer) : I'm enjoying it, but it's taking a long time for me to complete
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; my weekly tasks.
> A : **`You're learning the ropes.` Once you get the hang of it,** 
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I'm sure it won't take a long time.
> B : I hope so !
> 
> 💡 **get the hang of something : 감을 잡다. 방법을 터득하다.**
